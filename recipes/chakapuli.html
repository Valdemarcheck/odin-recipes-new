Chakapuli

Description

A lamb stew with herbs and wine is a dish that should be on the Georgian Easter table. Chakapuli is traditionally made with lamb, but you can also replace it with veal.

Ingredients

  • 1,5 kg lamb
  • 2 bunches Tarragon
  • 1 bunch Coriander
  • 1 bunch parsley
  • 1 bunch scallions
  • 100 g green sour plums (tkemali)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cup dry white wine
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Cut a meat into small pieces and place it in the saucepan.
  2. Add sour plums, finely chopped herbs and garlic.
  3. Season with salt and pour white wine in it.
  4. Cover the pan and place it on a medium-low heat.
  5. Cook for about one hour, until the meat is tender.
